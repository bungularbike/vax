Tutorial


Welcome to VAX. If you have played before you can skip this tutorial.


In VAX, your goal is to develop a vaccine and then distribute it around the world to end the pandemic.

VAX attempts to portray this in as realistic a manner as possible, dividing the game into phases that match an actual vaccine's development timeline.

You will have to conduct research and clinical trials, manufacture and deliver doses, manage your costs, and improve your relations with world governments.

This tutorial will teach you how VAX and its mechanics work.
